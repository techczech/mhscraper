From 1 August 2022, the [Travel Policy](https://staff.admin.ox.ac.uk/travelling-for-work/travel-policy) will apply to all business travel. This policy has replaced the travel guidance – whether for conferences, fieldwork, or placements – previously in place and introduce measures to help the University achieve its 2021 Environmental Sustainability Strategy targets.

### Be Aware

When planning to travel please allow enough time to complete the necessary pre-travel planning steps.

Complex trips abroad, working in remote areas or involving high-risk activities, may take several weeks or months to organise.
