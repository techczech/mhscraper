The course booking system is often referred to as "CoSy" at Oxford, referring to 'Course System'. It is a commercial system provided byÂ [Accessplanit](https://www.accessplanit.com/).

By clicking on the button below and signing in with your Single Sign On (SSO) credentials you will be taken to a personalised dashboard. From here you can search for courses, see what training you have undertaken, what you have coming up and pay for courses.

[BOOK A COURSE](https://cosy.ox.ac.uk/)
